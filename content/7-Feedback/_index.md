### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
My very first impression is that the working environment at FCAJ is incredibly friendly and welcoming. Everyone on the team is super enthusiastic; whenever I run into difficulties, they are always ready to help right away—even outside of working hours, and they never seem to mind at all. The workspace is neat and comfortable, which really helps me focus much better. If possible, I hope the company could organize a few more small social gatherings or team-bonding activities so that interns like us have more opportunities to connect and get to know everyone better.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin**  
I am extremely lucky to have a mentor who provides super detailed guidance. Whenever there is something I don't fully understand, they explain it thoroughly and constantly encourage me to ask questions. The admin team has also been incredibly supportive, helping me out with everything from procedures to documentation, which allowed me to quickly get used to the work. What I treasure the most is that my mentor always creates opportunities for me to research and try solving problems on my own first, rather than just handing over the answers right away. Thanks to this, I have become much more independent.

**3. Alignment Between Work and Academic Major**  
The tasks assigned to me fit perfectly with the knowledge I have been taught by my professors at school, while also opening up many completely new areas that I had never encountered before. This has helped me solidify my foundational knowledge while "pocketing" a lot of highly valuable real-world experience.

**4. Learning Opportunities & Skill Development**  
This internship has truly helped me "level up" many of my soft skills as well as technical skills: from using project management tools and teamwork coordination to professional communication. Besides that, the conversations and invaluable real-world experiences shared by my mentor have given me a much clearer vision regarding my future career orientation.

**5. Culture & Team Spirit**  
Our company's culture is genuinely positive. Everyone always respects one another; people are extremely serious when working but very joyful during breaks.

**6. Policies / Benefits for Interns**  
Everyone here makes it very easy for me to have flexible working hours whenever I get caught up with my exam schedules at school. For an intern like me, being able to participate in the company's internal training sessions is an incredibly huge plus.


### Other Questions
- **What are you most satisfied with during your internship?**  
  What I love the most is the highly supportive spirit of everyone here. Doing an internship here comes with absolutely no pressure or fear; I always feel respected and am given the opportunity to make mistakes and correct them.

- **What do you think the company needs to improve for future interns?**  
  I think the company could build a clearer task checklist or roadmap right from the very first week so that the next batches of interns won't feel overwhelmed and can catch up with the workflow more easily.

- **If recommending to friends, would you advise them to intern here? Why?**  
  It is a definite YES! I will immediately encourage my friends to apply because the environment here is just ideal for students who want real-world experience, serious learning, and guidance from highly dedicated mentors.


### Proposals & Desires
- **What proposals do you have to improve the internship experience?**  
  I hope to have more regular "1-on-1 Feedback" sessions (for instance, once every 2 weeks) between the mentor and the intern so that I can know what I did well and what areas I still need to improve.

- **Do you wish to continue with this program in the future?**  
  Yes, I really hope that after graduation, I will have the opportunity to continue accompanying this program for a long time to come.

- **Other feedback (free expression):**  
  I would like to send my most sincere thanks to everyone in the company, and especially to the FCAJ team for helping me out and giving me such a complete and meaningful internship! I wish our company continuous growth and even greater success in the future.
